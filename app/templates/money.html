In the construction industry, "BQ" typically refers to a "Bill of Quantities." A Bill of Quantities is a document used in the tendering process in construction projects. It provides a detailed list of the quantities and descriptions of the materials, labor, and other items required to complete a construction project based on the architectural and engineering drawings.
